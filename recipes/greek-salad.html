Greek Salad

Get ready for a zesty and tasty salad

This is a really easy way to whip up a healthy meal in no time. It comes from the typical Greek diet passed down for many generations. It's different from the leafy style of American salads and instead is a wonderful mix of chunky veggies and tofu substituting feta cheese. This recipe should only take about 20 minutes at most to prepare.

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • crushed pepper to taste

For the Salad

  • 2 cups quartered tomatoes
  • 1 sliced cucumber
  • 1 block pressed tofu
  • 1/2 thinly sliced red onion
  • 1 can pitted olives
  • 1 chunked bell pepper

How to make it

  1. Make the dressing ahead of time and allow the ingredients to mingle.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ingredients for the salad and mix
  3. Add the dressing and mix thoroughly.
  4. Voila! Go eat and Enjoy!
