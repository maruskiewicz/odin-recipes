Garlic Bread

A perfect companion

This savory and vegan garlic bread will have you asking for more. It's simple, tasty, and ready in no time. Pair with the greek salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 loaf bread sliced thick
  • 5 cloves minced garlic (or more!)

How to make it

  1. Heat the olive oil on medium-low.
  2. Add minced garlic to the olive oil.
  3. Let the garlic sautee until lightly golden (about 2 minutes).
  4. Take each slice and dab each side lightly onto the oil to coat.
  5. Set slices on a baking sheet and turn on your broiler.
  6. Be careful the bread can burn quickly! Check the bread every 30 seconds until toasted. This may take about 1-2 minutes
